# AI for Testing

This workshop is 10 hours of coaching through hands-on exercises and group conversations, sprinked with homework. It consists of four one hour sessions, and two three hour sessions. 

(1h) AI autopsy of conceptual architectures and tools with AI

(3h) Agentic testing: from test cases to telling problems, and from reporting problems to fixing problems. 

(1h) Reflections on agentic testing, and how to continue the journey.

(3h) Vibe coding: build an application and test it

(1h) Reflections on vibe coding, and how to continue the journey.

(1h) Demos and closing of open questions
