# Architecture-aware API testing

# Exploring programmatic interfaces

_Length: 1/2 day_

Target audience: quality engineers working with APIs and method signatures
Prerequisites: technically inclined, unafraid of code

The core question with testing is results coverage - are we finding the problems there are to find? With modern architectures and API-thinking, our chances of exploratory testing are greatly increased, but how do we do this systematically? APIs - or programmatic interfaces - can be found as the exposed ways of packaging our business logic but also on level of method signatures in unit testing. If 77% of production failures cn be reproduced by a unit test, the architecture awareness allows us to shift down on our testing, exploring on APIs and units, and increasing the opportunities of technical collaboration within our teams.
