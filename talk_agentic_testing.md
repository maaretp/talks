# Agentic testing

*Length: 30 min*

Agentic development assistants like GitHub Copilot, Claude Code and Codex, paired with direct codebase and application access with precisely crafted agent instructions, turn generic AI harnesses into focused testing engines. In this snapshot, we briefly walk through the story we take to clients:

Benchmark of small applications shows the gap between generic instructions and precisely crafted agent instructions with a skilled operator moves from 5 % to 100%. 
Existing development and testing teams find relevant problems they miss without agentic testing.
Packaging these agents with CC-BY-NC-SA license and granting an open and free license through engagements with us allows for fast-tracking local agentic testing through our services. 

We call our packaging with benchmarks CGI Test Intelligence Mesh. It’s an extension as well as an entry point to curated context for quality. Every agent that ‘just works’ is running on curated context. The quality of what the tools produce depends entirely on how well they're guided. Most teams are leaving significant value on the table without knowing it.
